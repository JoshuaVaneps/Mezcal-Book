Del Maguey

Tepextate

Hand-crafted on a tropical hillside surrounded by fruit trees and rose bushes in the village of Santa Maria Albarradas. Naturally fermented and twice distilled in wood-fired copper stills. Nose of banana taffy and spun sugar. Palate of passionfruit, marzipan and dark green herbs. Finishes with hints of cinnamon and honeysuckle.

  • ABV:

    45%

  • Agave:

    Tepextate

  • Aroma:

    Complex, earthy, hints of smoke

  • Pallet:

    roasted agave, herbal undertones, touch of sweetness

Tobalá

Hand-crafted on a tropical hillside surrounded by fruit trees and rose bushes in the village of Santa Maria Albarradas. Agave: Tobalá. Naturally fermented and twice distilled in wood-fired copper stills. Nose of fruit & florals, palate of mango, cinnamon & bright salinity with a long, creamy finish. ABV 48.

  • ABV:

    46%

  • Agave:

    Tobala

  • Aroma:

    Intense, floral, tropical fruit

  • Pallet:

    ripe agave, sweet spices, touch of minerality
